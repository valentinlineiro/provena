# Why Provena

Your career is knowledge. Documents are just representations.

Platforms change. Your source should remain yours.

## The problem

Most professionals maintain multiple versions of the same information — LinkedIn, résumés, personal websites, conference bios. These inevitably diverge. Maintenance becomes overhead. Inconsistencies erode credibility.

## The solution

A single canonical model. Deterministic projections.

```
Edit once. Represent everywhere.
```

## The principle

Knowledge, transformation, and representation are separate concerns.

- **Knowledge** — the canonical model of who you are and what you have done.
- **Transformation** — selecting and reshaping that knowledge for a specific context.
- **Representation** — writing the result for a specific medium.

No platform owns your identity. LinkedIn, recruiters, and conference committees consume projections. They do not define the source.

## The commitment

Provena will never lock your identity into a proprietary format, sell your data, or require a platform account.
